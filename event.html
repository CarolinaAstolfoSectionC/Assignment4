Welcome to Roll the Dice

Come watch some of the best known voice actors in the gaming industry play an intense Tabletop RPG two-shot campain!

Embark on an epic adventure where your imagination knows no bounds! Join us for a thrilling tabletop RPG session where our players will become heroes, facing perilous challenges, unraveling mysteries, and forging unforgettable tales.

Allana

You might know Allana from some big action style games. In this campain she plays a gnome with a great fondness for catapults and gears. A proficient alchemist, she needs money to pay her debts and reopen the family apothecary.

Gustavo

Gustavo is in a fan favorite car race game. This time he embodies a dwarf bard on a quest to recover a hometown relic of great importance to the population's safety.

Felipe

Felipe is most known from hir work as a VO director in various productions. On our adventure, he guides a drow sorcerer determined to escape his legacy in the Underworld.

Melina

Melina is a powerhouse in the industry. We are very lucky to have her play an aasimar barbarian with memory loss. The sound of thunder calls to her and brings flashes of unwanted memories.
